Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

1. Information We Collect

QR Handy Tools respects your privacy. We collect minimal information:

  • QR Code Data: Text, URLs, or information you enter is processed locally in your browser and not stored on our servers.
  • Usage Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site.
  • Advertising Data: Our advertising partners may collect data for personalized ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • Generate QR codes locally in your browser
  • Improve our service through analytics
  • Display relevant advertisements

3. Data Storage

Your QR code data is processed entirely in your browser and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. When you generate a QR code, the data remains on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics
  • Netlify: For website hosting

5. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics to improve user experience
  • Advertising personalization
  • Remembering your preferences

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request information about data collection

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website.

This privacy policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Google AdSense requirements.
